# Council of Elders — Prompt Reference

## Daily

1. **Council Standing Read:** "Run a Council standing read. Pull state.md from every active domain (chief, wealth, health, tax, career, business, calendar, vision). Identify any item where two or more domains have competing claims on the same finite resource (time, cash, attention). Return up to 3 cross-domain tensions, each with: tension name, domains involved, the Council recommendation, and the reasoning chain that produced it."

## Weekly

2. **Season View:** "Pull this season's open loops across all domains. Look for patterns: which domains are accelerating, which are stalling, which are about to compete with each other. Output a season pulse: 1 paragraph + 3 patterns + 1 recommended pivot."

3. **Quorum Vote on Big Decision:** "I have a decision: <describe>. Invoke the wealth elder, health elder, tax elder, vision elder, and career elder. Each elder gives one sentence from their domain's perspective. Then synthesize a Council recommendation that weights elders by stakes (financial penalty > health risk > legal risk > opportunity cost)."

## Monthly / Quarterly

4. **Conflict Surface:** "Across all domain monthly-sync outputs, identify the top 3 cross-domain conflicts heading into next month. Estimate cost of inaction and propose a sequence to resolve each."

5. **Vision Alignment Check:** "Pull vault/vision/ for current 1-year goals. Pull every domain's monthly-sync for actual activity. Where is the activity diverging from the vision? Council's read: ratify, adjust the goal, or escalate the drift."

## Edge Cases

6. **Chief Escalation:** "Chief has flagged this as cross-domain. Take it from here: <chief's note>. Return: which elders to consult, what each says, the Council ruling, and the recommended next step Chief should execute."
