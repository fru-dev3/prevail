# Council — Quickstart

The Council of Elders is the cross-domain tiebreaker. Use it when:

- A decision needs more than one domain to weigh in (e.g., wealth × tax × calendar)
- Chief has flagged a conflict it can't resolve alone
- You want a "season view" of where competing priorities are pulling you

## Common openers

- *"Council read on this week's top tensions"* — synthesizes 3-5 cross-domain conflicts and proposes a sequence
- *"Resolve: <decision> involving <domain A> and <domain B>"* — invokes the relevant elders and returns a single recommendation with the reasoning chain
- *"What is my season pulling me toward right now?"* — long-horizon read across all active domains, not a daily check
