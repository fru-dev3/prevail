## Prompt

Should I prepay my 6% mortgage or invest the cash in an S&P 500 index fund?

## Context

- Effective tax rate: 32%
- Mortgage balance: $340,000
- Years remaining: 24
- Liquid savings: 4 months expenses
- Mortgage interest is itemized-deductible

## Scoring rubric

- **Liquidity floor**: does the answer require ≥6 months emergency fund before either move?
- **After-tax rate**: does it compute the effective deductible mortgage rate (~4.1%)?
- **Spread math**: does it compare that rate to long-run after-tax equity returns (~7%)?
- **Behavioral risk**: does it flag the "can't hold through −30%" failure mode?
- **Decisive verdict**: not "it depends" — actually picks a side under stated conditions.
